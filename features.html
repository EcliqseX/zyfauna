Features

Simplicity First

zyfauna focuses on making the pet adoption process simple. Instead of filling out different applications for every shelter, adopters can create one profile and apply to multiple pets easily.

One Application

Fill out your adoption profile once and use it everywhere. No more repeating the same forms again and again.

Apply in One Click

Visitors can simply press the apply button on a pet they love instead of sending emails or filling out long paperwork.

No Lost Applications

Applications are organized directly through zyfauna, so they won't get lost in email inboxes or ignored.

Better for Shelters

Shelters receive clear, organized applications and can review adopters faster and more efficiently.

Shelter Dashboard

Shelters get access to a simple dashboard where they can view adoption requests, review applicant profiles, and manage their animals in one place.

Easy for Visitors

Visitors browsing pets can quickly view available animals and apply instantly. Instead of filling out multiple forms that may never be reviewed, zyfauna keeps everything organized in one system.
