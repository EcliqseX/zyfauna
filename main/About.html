About zyfauna

Adopting a pet should be exciting and simple. Unfortunately, many shelters require adopters to fill out different applications for every organization or animal they apply to. This can make the adoption process slow, repetitive, and frustrating.

zyfauna was created to solve this problem by introducing a universal adoption application. Instead of filling out multiple forms, adopters create one profile that can be used to apply to pets across multiple shelters.

Our goal is to simplify pet adoption while helping shelters receive organized applications and connect animals with the right families faster.

By reducing friction in the adoption process, zyfauna aims to help more animals find safe, loving homes.
